Sample WebGL

  • The blue/yellow quad is drawn using TRIANGLE_STRIP
  • The green triangle is drawn using TRIANGLES
  • The red/yellow circle is drawn using TRIANGLE_FAN
  • The cirle is draw at Z = 0 but the base of the triangle spans from negative Z (on the left, closer to the camera) to positive Z (on the right, farther from the camera), creating the effect that the triangle cuts through the circle
