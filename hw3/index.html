Description

I attempted to make a solar system of some sorts. Initially I created a rocket model followed by a satellite model. Once I completed the sphere object I used it for planets. In my sketch I used mostly ratios because nothing in my actual scene is proper size/scale. For artistic sake I made things relatively similar in size but try to create a recognizable scence which one could easily understand and visually find appealing. The sun is basically the origin and it spins on it's own Z-axis. I have 3 planets that orbit, Mercury, Venus, and Earth with moon. Mercury, Venus and Earth just orbit the origin too after translation of a sufficient amount. The moon orbits the Earth which in a sense would be orbiting the origin but I translate it to match the Earths current object coordinate frame. When the scene is first loaded a rocket is slowly heading towards the camera then stops. The satellite starts slightly off to the side and slowly moves but also stops just to make the scene seem whole as the planets continue to move in the background.
